Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

Smart Calculator respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information.

Information We Collect

Smart Calculator does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

The app does not require:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Login or registration
  • Location
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Camera access
  • Microphone access

Offline Functionality

Most features of Smart Calculator work completely offline.

The Currency Converter feature requires an internet connection only to download the latest exchange rates. Once downloaded, the latest exchange rates are stored locally on your device and can be used offline until they are updated again.

All calculations are performed locally on your device and are never sent to any server.

Calculation History

The app stores your recent calculation history locally on your device to improve your experience.

This information never leaves your device.

Permissions

Smart Calculator only requests permissions that are necessary for its features.

For example:

  • Storage access (only if required to save calculation result images)

The app does not access your personal files except when required for a feature you choose to use.

Data Sharing

Smart Calculator does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone.

Third-Party Services

The current version of Smart Calculator does not use third-party advertising or analytics services.

If future versions include services such as Google AdMob or other third-party providers, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

Children's Privacy

Smart Calculator is suitable for users of all ages.

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
